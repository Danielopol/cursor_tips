The Cursor AI Tips Directory is a community-driven collection of tips, tricks, and best practices for using Cursor AI effectively. It's designed to help developers at all skill levels get the most out of this powerful AI coding assistant.

You can submit your tip by clicking the "Contribute a Tip" button at the top of the page. Fill out the form with your tip details including title, description, categories, and content. Once submitted, your tip will be reviewed and added to the directory.

While we try to ensure all tips are accurate and helpful, the tips come from various community members with different experiences. The upvote/downvote system helps identify the most valuable tips. Always try a tip in a safe environment first if you're unsure.

The directory is updated regularly as new tips are submitted and approved. Follow our Twitter account to get notified when new tips are added or when significant updates occur.

The directory includes a wide range of tips, from basic usage for beginners to advanced techniques for power users. Tips are categorized into groups like Prompting, Workflow, Debugging, and more to help you find exactly what you need.
